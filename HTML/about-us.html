THE LEGEND OF TANAKA GUITAR

George Tanaka was a young man hustling in the 2010s as he was adrift in post-recession Indonesia. He eventually found a career in buying, trading, and selling guitars out of his home. However, it was only until 2021, when he managed to build a 125 meters squared shack and founded Tanaka Guitar in Salatiga, Central Java.

Over time, the business grew and Tanaka partnered with his long-time friend, Tatan Aka. Eventually, Tanaka Guitar opened more stores in Tangerang, Cirebon, Semarang, and Surabaya. The main shop even expanded from the measly shack into an extravagant guitar exhibition.

Nowadays, Tanaka Guitar has over 30 shops across Indonesia, housing over 50,000 guitars. This collection varies from personal custom models to the best many brands can offer. We have built a loyal, customer base and we will guarantee you a once in a lifetime deal here, specified for the music lover in you.
